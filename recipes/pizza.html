Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a crispy or chewy dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. From classic Margherita to fully loaded supreme, pizza offers endless possibilities for flavor and creativity.

Homemade pizza lets you experiment with fresh ingredients and create a pizza exactly to your taste. Perfect for family dinners, parties, or a cozy night in, pizza is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, or other desired toppings
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
