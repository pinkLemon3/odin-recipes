Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a crispy or chewy dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. From classic Margherita to fully loaded supreme, pizza offers endless possibilities for flavor and creativity.

Homemade pizza lets you experiment with fresh ingredients and create a pizza exactly to your taste. Perfect for family dinners, parties, or a cozy night in, pizza is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, or other desired toppings
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add your chosen toppings evenly on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the pizza rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, slice, and serve hot.

Enjoy your pizza!
